# Sound tracker

Trending TikTok sounds have a useful life of roughly 48–72 hours from the
moment they break out. After that, the algorithmic boost is gone and the
sound reads as saturated. Log sounds as you spot them, set a ride-or-skip
deadline, and use the deadline to force the decision.

## How to use this

1. When you spot a sound twice in one scrolling session, check it on the
   sounds page. If it has under ~10k uses and is climbing, it is inside
   the window. Log it below.
2. Set the deadline to 48 hours from the moment you spotted it.
3. Decide ride or skip within 24 hours. Skipped sounds expire; ridden
   sounds need a draft inside the 48-hour deadline.
4. After the deadline, mark posted (with the link) or expired.

## Decision rules

- **Ride if:** the sound fits your format without contortion, you can
  draft inside 24 hours, and the concept does not require new
  equipment or a brand re-shoot.
- **Skip if:** the sound is already at 100k+ uses (window closed), it
  fits awkwardly, or you would need to abandon a planned post you
  believe in more.
- **Default to skip on close calls.** A forced trend post tends to
  underperform the format content you would have made instead.

## Account-type reminder

If you are on a Business account, your sound options are limited to the
Commercial Music Library, which lags real trends by days or weeks. You
cannot legally use the trending tracks you spot on FYP. Switch to a
Creator account in Settings → Account if commercial intent and trending
sound access both matter (Business is also not eligible for Creator
Rewards). See `references/sounds.md` and `references/anti-patterns.md`.
