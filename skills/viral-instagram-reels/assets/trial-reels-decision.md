# Trial Reels decision sheet

One page. Fill it in per Reel. Default to a trial if cold reach is the
goal and the account is eligible.

```
REEL WORKING TITLE:
PRIMARY GOAL:        (cold reach / follower nurture / time-sensitive moment)
ACCOUNT FOLLOWERS:   (under 1K = no trial available)
ACCOUNT IS PUBLIC:   [ ] yes  [ ] no
ACCOUNT TYPE:        (Creator / Business)
```

## Eligibility gate

- [ ] Account has at least about 1,000 followers.
- [ ] Account is public.

If either is unchecked, you can't trial. Skip to publish, focus on the
0 to 1K slog. (See `references/trial-reels.md` for the mechanic.)

## Goal gate

- [ ] The goal of this Reel is cold reach (non-followers).
- [ ] You are uncertain whether the hook lands.
- [ ] You're not certain the topic fits cold audiences.
- [ ] You want a clean read before committing the Reel to the grid.

If all four are checked, run a trial. The flop is free, the win is
confirmed.

If you checked one or fewer, the Reel may be a publish, not a trial.

## Publish-direct (skip the trial)

Publish directly without trialling when one of these is true:

- [ ] Primarily for existing followers (BTS, community moment, thank-you).
- [ ] Time-sensitive in a way that needs the grid right now (product
      drop, calendar moment, live event).
- [ ] Part of a series your followers expect to see.

Followers don't see trials, so a trial wastes the audience that this
Reel was actually for.

## After the trial (24 to 72 hour read)

Compare against your own prior trials, not against follower-feed posts.

- [ ] **Numbers above your trial baseline.** Share to everyone now.
      Don't wait for auto-share. Early momentum matters.
- [ ] **Numbers okay but not obvious.** Let Instagram auto-share at
      72 hours. Passive yes, lets the platform's own judgement decide.
- [ ] **Numbers below baseline, hook held.** Topic likely didn't fit
      cold audiences. Don't publish. Save the footage, recut the topic
      framing, re-trial.
- [ ] **Numbers below baseline, hook failed (high skip rate).** Don't
      publish. Recut the opening three seconds and re-trial.

## Quick decisions

- Cold reach goal, eligible, uncertain about hook -> trial.
- Time-sensitive grid moment -> publish.
- Follower-only nurture -> publish (or skip Reels entirely).
- Under 1K followers -> publish, you can't trial.
- Trial converted strongly -> share to everyone immediately, don't wait.
- Trial flopped -> don't publish, recut the opening or the topic frame,
  re-trial.
