# Reels hook checklist

Run any candidate Reels hook through this. A strong hook clears most of
these. If it fails any of the first four, rewrite it before going further.
The three-second cliff is unforgiving on Reels.

## Pass / fail criteria

- [ ] **Lands in about 2 seconds when said out loud.** No "hey guys," no
      logo intro, no slow zoom.
- [ ] **First spoken word is the most interesting word in the line.** Cut
      anything before it.
- [ ] **Works on mute.** Three to five bold words on the first frame
      carry the hook for sound-off viewers.
- [ ] **Names a concrete viewer, number, or stake.** No vague benefits
      ("level up your skincare"), no abstract promises.
- [ ] **Opens a loop the body actually closes**, or names a stake the
      payoff actually addresses. No orphan clickbait.
- [ ] **Signals a shareable payoff.** The viewer can already think
      "I know someone who needs to see this" by the third second.
- [ ] **First frame composition holds the hook.** Face, reveal, motion,
      or sharp contrast. Not a flat static product shot.
- [ ] **No third-party app UI in the frame.** No TikTok timer, no CapCut
      overlay, no screen-recording chrome.
- [ ] **On-screen text sits clear of Instagram's UI.** Not in the bottom
      fifth where captions sit, not in the top fifth where account info
      sits.
- [ ] **Topic is legible from the hook alone.** A stranger can name what
      this Reel is about in three seconds, without sound.

## Fast upgrades when a hook is weak

- Add a number or a specific time / dollar figure.
- Name the exact viewer ("if you [specific situation]...").
- Cut everything before the first interesting word.
- Swap a vague benefit for a concrete result.
- Turn a statement into a withheld reveal ("I won't say the brand, but...").
- Pair the spoken hook with a contrast on screen (text says one thing,
  visual shows another).
- Rewrite the on-screen text as a three-to-five word headline, not a
  sentence.

## Reels-specific failures to look for

- Hook works with sound, fails on mute. (Sound-off viewers scroll.)
- Hook promises something the body can't deliver. (Tanks sends, the
  metric that actually buys cold reach.)
- Hook is muddy across topics. (Topic legibility is what converts views
  into follows on Reels.)
