# On-screen text spec

One-page editor brief for the burned-in text layer. Fill it in once per
video. See `references/on-screen-text.md` for the rules.

```
WORKING TITLE:
PLATFORM:      (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)
ASPECT:        9:16, 1080x1920

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HEADLINE (large, static, top, in the safe zone)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
COPY:          [5–8 words. the hook line. mirrors the spoken hook.]
POSITION:      top-center, inside the platform safe zone
FONT:          [Montserrat Bold / TikTok Sans / Futura Bold / Archivo Black]
WEIGHT:        800+ (extra bold)
SIZE:          [headline pt]
COLOR:         white with black outline (or inverse for light backgrounds)
HOLD:          first frame to ~3s, then ride if relevant

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BODY CAPTIONS (karaoke / built word-by-word as voiceover speaks)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SOURCE:        burned-in (do not rely on platform auto-captions)
STYLE:         karaoke highlight (color or scale) on each word as spoken
POSITION:      center or bottom-center, ABOVE the platform caption rail
FONT:          [same family as headline, or matched sans-serif]
WEIGHT:        700+
SIZE:          22–28pt body
COLOR:         white with black outline; highlight color [hex]
TIMING:        one word or short phrase per beat, in sync with audio
ACCURACY:      verbatim of the spoken line; fix any transcript errors

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SAFE ZONES (keep critical text out of these)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TIKTOK:        top 130px, bottom 440–484px, left 60px, right 120px
REELS:         bottom ~600px, right ~150px
SHORTS:        bottom ~30% of frame

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THREE-SURFACE SEO CHECK (caption + on-screen + voiceover agree)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PRIMARY KEYWORD:           [word]
APPEARS IN HEADLINE:       [ ]
APPEARS IN BODY CAPTIONS:  [ ]
APPEARS IN VOICEOVER:      [ ]
APPEARS IN WRITTEN CAPTION: [ ]
```

## Editor's quick pass

- [ ] Headline is readable in one glance on a phone screen.
- [ ] Headline sits inside the safe zone, not under the UI.
- [ ] Body captions are burned in, not platform auto-captions.
- [ ] Body captions match the voiceover word-for-word.
- [ ] Karaoke pace tracks the spoken beat, not a static dump.
- [ ] Contrast holds against every background shot in the video.
- [ ] The primary keyword shows up in the headline and body.
