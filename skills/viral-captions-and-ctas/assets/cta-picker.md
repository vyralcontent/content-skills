# CTA picker

Pick one primary action per video, matched to the content type. Use the
matrix to land on the right shape, then steal the phrasing. See
`references/ctas-that-work.md` for the reasoning.

## Matrix

| Content type | Primary action | Why it fits |
|---|---|---|
| Tutorial / how-to | Save | Viewer will need it later |
| List / round-up | Save | Reference content |
| Recommendation / review | Send | "You need to see this" travels |
| Story / personal | Send | Specific person comes to mind |
| Demo / before-after | Save or send | Recipe vs "look at this" |
| Opinion / hot take | Comment with stance | Real debate fuels the section |
| Cliffhanger to long-form | Watch full video | The Short is the trailer |
| Pure entertainment | None (let it loop) | Asking taxes a working video |

## Phrasings to steal

### Save (tutorial, list, how-to)

- "Save this before your next [specific moment]."
- "Save this for the next time you [predictable future situation]."
- "If you're saving these, the third one is the one nobody does."
- Implicit, said mid-video at a peak: "if you're writing this down..."

### Send (recommendation, story, review)

- "Send this to your friend who keeps [specific behaviour]."
- "Send to the one person who's been asking you about [thing]."
- "Your [editor / dog walker / mum / partner] will know what to do
  with this."
- Implicit: "your skincare friend will thank you for this."

### Comment with stance (opinion, hot take)

- "Tell me I'm wrong about [specific claim]."
- "Which one would you pick, and why?"
- "What's the dumbest thing you've spent money on for this?"
- The test: are you asking for the **content** of a real opinion, or
  just the **act** of commenting? Stick to content.

### Watch full video (cliffhanger from a Short)

- "Full breakdown of why this works is on my channel."
- "I cut the part that explains why. It's the [length] version
  pinned to my profile."
- The Short still has to feel satisfying. Tease without value reads
  as an ad.

## Don'ts (cut on sight)

- "Like for part 2"
- "Comment YES for the link"
- "Tag a friend who needs this"
- "Follow for more [topic] tips"
- "Drop a heart if you agree"
- "Share with 5 friends"
- Any stack of three or more asks in one breath

Each of these buys a low-weight action and pays a reach penalty. Run
the rewrite through `bait-check.md`.

## Placement

- Layered is best: implicit prompt at the 70% mark, explicit prompt
  near the end.
- Data-driven version: open Insights or Analytics, find the actual
  drop-off, place the primary CTA about 5 seconds before it.
- Never lead with a CTA. CTAs land when they ride a value moment.
