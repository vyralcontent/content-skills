# Shorts to long-form bridge

Fill in every slot. The bridge works best when at least three of the four
surfaces (in-video callout, pinned comment, end-card label, channel
trailer + Shorts tab consistency) fire on the same Short. A Short with
none of these can perform and still earn zero long-form clicks because
there is nowhere to send people.

```
SHORT TOPIC:                  (one sentence)
SHORT'S MAIN PAYOFF:          (what the viewer learns or sees)

LONG-FORM DESTINATION
─────────────────────────────────────────
EXACT TITLE:                  (the long-form on the channel)
CHAPTER / TIMESTAMP:          (the specific part the Short teases, if any)
WHY THIS LONG-FORM:           (one sentence on why the Short's viewer wants it)
WHAT'S IN IT NOT IN SHORT:    (the concrete extra; "longer version" does not earn clicks)

IF NO LONG-FORM EXISTS YET:
  [ ] Write the long-form first, then post the Short.
  [ ] OR accept this Short has no funnel CTA and let it earn reach.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SURFACE 1: IN-VIDEO CALLOUT (last ~5 seconds of the Short)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SPOKEN LINE:
  "If you want the full [topic], the [length-or-detail] version is the
   [exact title] video on my channel."

ON-SCREEN TEXT (safe zone, paired with the spoken line):
  "Full breakdown: [short title]"

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SURFACE 2: PINNED COMMENT (post immediately after upload, then pin)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TEMPLATE:
  "Full [topic] video here, with [one specific extra not in this
   Short]: [link]"

EXAMPLE OF A WEAK PINNED COMMENT (avoid):
  "Subscribe for more like this!"

EXAMPLE OF A STRONG PINNED COMMENT (use):
  "Full 14-minute breakdown with every product I used and timestamps
   for each step: [link]"

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SURFACE 3: END-CARD / FINAL FRAME
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FINAL FRAME LABEL:
  "Full video: [short title]"

LOOP DESIGN:
  [ ] Final frame matches opening frame (loop fires)
  [ ] Final beat names the long-form (callout lands)
  [ ] Both at once

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SURFACE 4: CHANNEL CONTEXT (set this up before the Short pops)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CHANNEL TRAILER:              (currently set to which long-form?)
  [ ] Trailer is the long-form this Short bridges to, OR
  [ ] Trailer is on the same topic cluster as this Short

SHORTS TAB STATE:
  [ ] Pinned Shorts (2 to 3) are on the same topic as this Short
  [ ] Recent Shorts (last 5) are topically consistent
  [ ] No off-topic Shorts scheduled to post in the next 48 hours

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
POST-UPLOAD TRACKING (read in Studio after 72 hours)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VVSA:                         (target 70%+ for the Short to even get tested)
ENGAGED VIEWS:                (the real number, not the inflated View count)
CLICKS TO LONG-FORM:          (Studio > traffic source > "From a Short")
WHICH SURFACE DROVE CLICKS:   (in-video / pinned / end-card / channel page)
NEXT MOVE:                    (what to double down on for the next Short)
```
