# Shorts VVSA checklist

Run every Short through this before upload. The first six items decide
VVSA (the swipe-or-stay gate). The next four decide whether the Short
clears the algorithm beyond the first 3 seconds. Fail any of the first
six and rewrite the opening before posting.

## First 3 seconds (the VVSA gate)

- [ ] **First frame is the hook.** No logo, no animated intro, no
      welcome-back, no establishing shot. The first frame earns the second
      frame.
- [ ] **First spoken line lands inside ~1.5 seconds.** Say it out loud. If
      it stumbles or takes longer than 2 seconds, cut it.
- [ ] **On-screen text on frame 1.** Three to five words, large, high
      contrast.
- [ ] **Hook reads as a hook on mute.** The on-screen text alone makes a
      viewer want the next beat.
- [ ] **Hook promises a specific payoff** the body actually delivers.
- [ ] **No "subscribe" or "hey guys"** in the first 3 seconds. Ever.

## Body and structure

- [ ] **Escalation around 5 to 8 seconds.** A turn, a stakes raise, or a
      specificity injection. Not a plateau.
- [ ] **Cut or shot reset every 2 to 3 seconds.** Eyes never settle into
      "I've seen enough."
- [ ] **Payoff lands before the natural drop-off** for the length. Don't
      bury it in the last quarter.
- [ ] **Last beat loops back to the first** (visual, audio, or question
      loop). Designed, not accidental.

## Format and platform compliance

- [ ] **Aspect ratio is 9:16 vertical** (or square). Not 16:9 with bars.
- [ ] **Captions are in the safe zone** (middle third of the frame).
      Nothing important in the top ~20% or bottom ~25%.
- [ ] **No TikTok or other-platform watermark.** Cropped or removed.
- [ ] **Captions burned into the video.** Not relying on auto-generated
      subtitles alone.

## CTA and funnel

- [ ] **CTA is the long-form bridge, not "subscribe."** Names the
      specific long-form by topic.
- [ ] **Pinned comment prepared** with the long-form link and a one-line
      reason to click.
- [ ] **Channel's Shorts tab is topically consistent** with this Short
      (especially if you expect it to pop).

## Monetization choices (optional, depending on goal)

- [ ] **Original audio** if optimizing for revenue, or if the channel is
      under ~50k subs.
- [ ] **No Content ID claim** that could block a 60s+ Short globally.
- [ ] **Length matches the goal.** 30 to 45s for most cases; sub-15s only
      if the payoff is genuinely that fast.

## Post-upload

- [ ] **Read VVSA first**, not raw views. Under 60% means the opening
      failed.
- [ ] **Read the retention curve** for cliff shape (3s cliff, slow bleed,
      late cliff). Map to a fix.
- [ ] **Read Engaged Views**, not Views, for any revenue or YPP claim.
