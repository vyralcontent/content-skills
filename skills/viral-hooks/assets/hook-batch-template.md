# Hook batch template

Use this when generating six to ten hooks for a single video idea.
Aim for three or more archetypes represented. One row per hook. Keep
the verbal line under 2 seconds when said out loud.

See `references/hook-archetypes.md` for the archetype menu.

```
VIDEO IDEA:         [WHAT IS THE VIDEO ABOUT]
NICHE:              [NICHE]
AUDIENCE:           [WHO IS THIS FOR, BE SPECIFIC]
PLATFORM:           (TikTok / Reels / Shorts / mix)
GOAL:               (reach / saves / clicks to long-form / conversion)
CONSTRAINTS:        (no-face, brand voice, sponsor, etc.)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
#  | ARCHETYPE       | VERBAL LINE             | ON-SCREEN TEXT  | FIRST FRAME           | RATIONALE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1  | [ARCHETYPE]     | [SPOKEN LINE, <2s]      | [3-7 WORDS]     | [WHAT THE EYE SEES]   | [ONE LINE: WHY THIS]
2  | [ARCHETYPE]     | [SPOKEN LINE, <2s]      | [3-7 WORDS]     | [WHAT THE EYE SEES]   | [ONE LINE: WHY THIS]
3  | [ARCHETYPE]     | [SPOKEN LINE, <2s]      | [3-7 WORDS]     | [WHAT THE EYE SEES]   | [ONE LINE: WHY THIS]
4  | [ARCHETYPE]     | [SPOKEN LINE, <2s]      | [3-7 WORDS]     | [WHAT THE EYE SEES]   | [ONE LINE: WHY THIS]
5  | [ARCHETYPE]     | [SPOKEN LINE, <2s]      | [3-7 WORDS]     | [WHAT THE EYE SEES]   | [ONE LINE: WHY THIS]
6  | [ARCHETYPE]     | [SPOKEN LINE, <2s]      | [3-7 WORDS]     | [WHAT THE EYE SEES]   | [ONE LINE: WHY THIS]
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

TOP PICK:           # [N] because [REASON]
BACKUP TO A/B:      # [N]
FILM PLAN:          shoot top pick + one backup; read first-3s retention to choose
```

Archetype shorthand to use in the grid:

- `CONTRARIAN`     break the consensus
- `INVESTIGATOR`   "I found a hidden thing"
- `EXPERIMENTER`   "I did X to find out Y"
- `TEACHER`        "X person hit Y using Z method"
- `MAGICIAN`       visual-led, "look at this"
- `FORTUNETELLER`  near-future stakes claim
- `WITHHOLD`       show the result, hold the product
- `STAKES`         "stop doing X if you want Y"
- `CALLOUT`        "if you [situation], this is for you"
- `COST-CONFESS`   "I spent [cost] so you don't have to"
- `IN-MEDIA-RES`   start mid-action, explain after
- `NUMBER-LEAD`    big concrete number first

Notes:

- One archetype per row, no two rows on the same archetype unless the
  execution is meaningfully different.
- If two rows look like rewordings of each other, cut one and replace
  with a new archetype.
- Pair this with `assets/hook-checklist.md` once the batch is drafted.
